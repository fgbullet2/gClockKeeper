gClock Keeper

This program is a Web Based Daily Time Record (DTR). It can log your Time-in, Time-out and Date. You view or summarized your logs in week, month or the date you selected.

Instruction Manual

To access this program:

1. Open a Internet Explorer
2. Put in the URL bar this address:
  • For gClock Keeper Las Pinas = http://gclockkeeper-lpc
  • For gClock Keeper Cebu = http://gclockkeeper-cebu
  • For gClock Keeper CDO = http://gclockkeeper-cdo
  • For gClock Keeper Zamboanga = http://gclockkeeper-zam
  • For gClock Keeper Iloilo = http://gclockkeeper-ilo
3. Select your name then enter your password. If you are not registered, click the link on the "New User". Fill up the form properly, select your designated department, then click Submit. Then click the link below to get back to Main Menu.
4. Once you are already registered, you can now log-in to your personal gClock Logger.

To Log-in/Log-out:

1. To Log-In your time, select the "Time In" button, then click "Submit it".
Note: You can only leave notes when you're about to log-in.
2. To Log-Out, select the "Time Out" button, then click "Submit it".

To View your Logs:

1. You can now view the Date, Time In, Time out, and your notes. You can also view selected Time Logs by choosing the date you want.
2. To Log-Out, simply click the "Log-Out" button on the "Quick Links" then close the Internet Explorer.

Note: This program is still under development but already capable of doing the basics of a Daily Time Record. Please email me any bugs/comment you will notice.
